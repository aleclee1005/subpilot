Features

📝

Auto-load Subtitles

Fetch and display the full transcript in a side panel — no need to enable CC manually.

⚡

Real-time Highlight

The currently playing line is highlighted as the video plays. Click any line to jump to that moment.

✨

Custom AI Instructions

Add a prompt like "Summarize in 500 words" or "Create a 10-slide outline" before sending.

🚀

Auto-submit

Optional toggle to automatically click the send button after text is inserted into the AI chat.

Supported AI Services

ChatGPT Claude Gemini DeepSeek Dola Qwen

How to Use

1

Open any YouTube video with subtitles.

2

Click Subtitles in the side panel — the transcript loads automatically.

3

Click Send to AI, type optional instructions, and select your AI services.

4

Click Send — selected AI tabs open with subtitles pre-filled and ready to go.
